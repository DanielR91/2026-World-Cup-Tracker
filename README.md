# 🏆 2026 World Cup Tracker

Welcome to the **2026 World Cup Tracker**, a premium tactical insight dashboard designed for football fans following the tournament's live action, broadcast channels, group stages, and historical metrics.

🔗 **[Open the Live Dashboard](https://2026-world-cup-tracker.vercel.app/)**

---

## 🌟 Overview

The 2026 World Cup Tracker is a high-performance web dashboard that brings real-time analytics, standings, and UK broadcast schedules right to your fingertips. Built with a sleek dark slate theme (`#0f172a`), the interface operates at lightning speeds with zero bloat—designed to keep you updated on the go.

---

## ⚡ Core Features

### 📺 Live Match Ticker Ribbon
- **Real-Time Match Cards:** A scrolling ribbon at the top of the page displaying live match times, real-time scorelines, and scheduled matchups.
- **UK TV Broadcast Badges:** Each card highlights the corresponding UK channel (**BBC One / iPlayer** or **ITV1 / ITVX**) dynamically updated using live web listings.
- **FIFA Standard Display:** Team cards are cleanly structured using official country flag emojis and bold 3-letter FIFA codes (e.g. `🏴󠁧󠁢󠁥󠁮󠁧󠁿 ENG vs GHA 🇬🇭`).

### 📊 Real-Time Tournament Statistics
- **Discipline & Goal Trackers:** Instantly view total goals, yellow cards, and red cards accumulated across all completed matches.
- **Per-Match Metrics:** Average stats are calculated down to the decimal (e.g., *Avg: 2.41 per match*), keeping you informed on the tournament's scoring rate and play style.

### ⚽ Symmetrical 2x2 Leaderboards Grid
Keep track of individual and national honors with four comprehensive rankings:
1. **Top Scorers:** View the tournament's leading goal scorers alongside their respective nation flags.
2. **Top Playmakers:** Track assists to see who is pulling the strings in midfield.
3. **Top Clean Sheets:** Spot the tournament's most impenetrable defenses.
4. **Most Aggressive Teams:** A weighted ranking (1pt per Yellow Card, 3pts per Red Card) showing the teams bringing the most chaos to the pitch.

### 🔍 Interactive Team Selector Panel
Select any of the 48 participating countries to unlock a custom panel containing:
- **Team Stats Summary:** Total goals, yellow cards, and red cards accumulated by your chosen nation.
- **Recent/Upcoming Matches:** Detailed card listings for recent results and upcoming matches, complete with UK broadcast badges, stadium locations, and seating capacities.
- **Live Group Standings:** A fully responsive standings table showcasing the chosen team's group letter, points, and goal difference, with the selected team highlighted for quick comparison.
